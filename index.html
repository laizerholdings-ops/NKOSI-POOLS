Service Matrix

Technical Capability

New Construction

Execution of turnkey aquatic projects from excavation to structural finishing. We leverage reinforced concrete and gunite techniques to deliver pools that withstand soil movement and environmental stressors, customized to unique architectural blueprints.

Maintenance

Advanced mechanical auditing and water chemistry management. Our maintenance protocols involve high-precision balancing of pH, alkalinity, and sanitizer levels, alongside regular filter cleaning and equipment inspections to ensure 24/7 crystal-clear clarity.

Renovations

Resurfacing and structural revitalization for aging pools. We specialize in marble plastering, high-grade mosaic retiling, and technical leak detection, breathing new life into older structures while upgrading them to modern technical standards.

Solar Integration

Sustainable thermal energy deployment designed to extend your swimming season. We install eco-friendly solar collector systems that harness renewable energy, significantly reducing heating costs while maintaining optimal water temperature year-round.

Safety Systems

Installation of premium, high-tension safety covers and architectural security barriers. Our solutions are designed for maximum risk mitigation—protecting children and pets while doubling as effective debris and evaporation shields.

Logistics

Industrial-grade supply chain management for pool components. From SABS-approved pumps and salt chlorinators to high-performance chemical sanitizers, we source and deploy only the most reliable hardware in the South African market.
